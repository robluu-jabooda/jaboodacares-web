CRA Eligibility Statement

Jabooda Cares qualifies for CRA consideration under the Community Development Investment category. We are a CHDO-designated 501(c)(3) developing affordable housing at 30% AMI in LMI census tracts within King County, Washington. Grants, equity investments, and construction financing through Jabooda Cares generate CRA credit for regulated financial institutions under 12 CFR 25.23(a). Our affiliated general contractor, Jabooda Construction (WMBE-certified), allows us to capture construction margin that makes 30% AMI projects financially viable without additional subsidy.

Certifications & Designations

Institutional credibility, documented

Our certifications are verifiable through the issuing agencies. Compliance documentation available upon request.

CHDO
Community Housing Development Organization

HUD-designated CHDO status enables Jabooda Cares to receive HOME Investment Partnership funds and NHTF allocations. Designates us as a mission-driven affordable housing developer with the capacity to develop, own, and manage housing.

Issuing Authority: HUD / WA Commerce
WMBE
Women & Minority Business Enterprise

State-certified WMBE status through the Washington State Office of Minority & Women's Business Enterprises. Applies to both Jabooda Cares and affiliated Jabooda Construction, supporting supplier diversity and equity goals in public contracting.

Issuing Authority: WA OMWBE
501(c)(3)
Tax-Exempt Public Charity

IRS-recognized public charity. Contributions to Jabooda Cares are tax-deductible to the full extent permitted by law. Corporate grants and CRA investments qualify for charitable deduction treatment where applicable.

EIN: 99-4589825
AANHPI
By-and-For AANHPI Organization

Washington State Commerce By-and-For assessment completed. Jabooda Cares is led by and primarily serves Asian American, Native Hawaiian, and Pacific Islander communities in King County, addressing culturally specific displacement and housing insecurity.

Issuing Authority: WA Commerce
Partnership Process

Three steps to a partnership

We keep this straightforward. Most CRA conversations move from introduction to letter of intent in under 30 days.

Step 01
20-Minute Introduction

Schedule a call with our development team. We'll share our current project pipeline, CRA documentation package, and discuss where your institution's community development priorities align with our work.

Step 02
Due Diligence Package

We provide a complete CRA documentation package: 501(c)(3) determination letter, CHDO designation, WMBE certification, audited financials, project pro formas, census tract confirmation, and board-approved conflict of interest policy.

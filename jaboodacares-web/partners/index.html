Partner With Us

Jabooda Cares is building a pathway to homeownership for AANHPI families in Seattle's Rainier Valley. We partner with lenders, employers, community organizations, and government agencies to close the racial homeownership gap.

Government

Public Sector

City of Seattle Office of Housing, Sound Transit, King County DCHS, and Washington State Commerce fund the programs that make below-market homeownership possible.

Financial

Lending Partners

CDFI lenders and community banks provide down payment assistance, shared equity financing, and culturally responsive mortgage products for first-generation homebuyers.

Community

Community Organizations

AANHPI-serving nonprofits, culturally specific housing counselors, and neighborhood organizations connect families to homeownership readiness programs.

CRA Eligibility Statement

Jabooda Cares qualifies for CRA consideration under the Community Development Investment category. We are a CHDO-designated 501(c)(3) developing affordable housing in LMI census tracts within King County, Washington. Grants, equity investments, and construction financing through Jabooda Cares generate CRA credit for regulated financial institutions under 12 CFR 25.23(a). Our affiliated general contractor, Jabooda Construction, holds OMWBE state WMBE certification, enabling the Jabooda team to deliver on both the community mission and the construction capacity needed to reach lower AMI levels.

Certifications & Designations

Institutional credibility, documented

Community Housing Development Organization

HUD-designated CHDO status enables Jabooda Cares to receive HOME Investment Partnership funds and NHTF allocations. Designates us as a mission-driven affordable housing developer with the capacity to develop, own, and manage housing.

Partnership Process

Three steps to a partnership

We keep this straightforward. Most CRA conversations move from introduction to letter of intent in under 30 days.

Step 01
20-Minute Introduction

Schedule a call with our development team. We'll share our current project pipeline, CRA documentation package, and discuss where your institution's community development priorities align with our work.

Step 02
Due Diligence Package

We provide a complete CRA documentation package: 501(c)(3) determination letter, CHDO designation, Jabooda Construction WMBE certification, audited financials, project pro formas, census tract confirmation, and board-approved conflict of interest policy.

Step 03
Grant or Investment

Capacity grants ($25K–$150K), project-specific grants, equity investments in LIHTC deals, or construction financing. We work with your CRA officer to structure the relationship for maximum credit value and mission impact. FHLB AHP sponsorship available through member banks.
